about me/

Hello! My name is Karin Kasai. I am a 2nd-year graphic design student at Humber college. Born in Japan, raised in Tokyo and now happily live in Toronto,Canada.

My goal is to make someone’s live better through my creativity and identity.

I had worked at E-commerce company as an administrator/designer. My job was to operate fashion website from product registration to design banner, webpage, newsletter and marketing. Although I was happy with my position, I realized I want to be a creator rather than operator. Hence, I have decided to focus my career as a graphic designer.

Even since I was a child, art has been important part in my life. Whenever I was doing art works or designing something, I liked the moment I was working on it. It makes me focused and made me free from pressures of all things I had stuck in my mind. Art has freedom and I get to choose whichever way I like. I think that is the beauty of art.

Moreover, I like the feeling of satisfaction after I finally got to see the outcome. Sometimes it doesn’t turn out good as I expected. But even so, that is also part of the path to the success for next outcome. Thus, it is worth the effort.

However, now is the time for me to utilize those passions for arts for other people to make their life even better through my design. My goal is to design and establish identity for people who doesn’t have easy access to internet in order to convey their messages. There are so many ways to express identity in this world. As a designer, I believe my mission is to create the right direction to build a strong identity that can tell the story and color of itself.
